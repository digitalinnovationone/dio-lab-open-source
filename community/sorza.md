### About me:

When I was thirteen years old, I had my first contact with software development through a technical course, where I fell completely in love with it. A few years later, at the age of 18, I started my degree in Systems Analysis and Development and completed it at the age of 21.

Unfortunately, adversities prevented me from entering an internship or entry-level job and this dream became increasingly distant.

Today, after 8 years, after all the storm has finally calmed down, the flame of the teenage dream is still alive and shines brighter than ever!

Today, I'm racing against time to update myself on the best practices and technologies available. I know that not having prior experience is a complication, but I believe that a dream only dies when we stop dreaming!
