# Marcos Alves

After completing an extensive Python course for linguists, I gathered the courage to leave my academic path and pursue what my heart believes to be the best choice. Through my self-directed programming studies, my interest expanded into the mobile realm. Since then, my focus has been on developing skills to grow as a mobile software engineer.
