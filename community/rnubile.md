# Rhyan Lelis

Master in the Graduate Program in Biosciences and Biotechnology Applied to Pharmacy at the São Paulo State University “Júlio de Mesquita Filho” (Unesp), researcher in the Department of Bioprocess and Biotechnology Engineering and in the Department of Pharmaceutical Sciences. Experience in industrial microbiology, industrial enzymology, fermentation, biorefinery, production of bioactives, biofuels and biosurfactants, extraction and purification, bioreactors, experiment planning and process optimization. Graduated in Biotechnology from the Federal University of Alfenas (Unifal), with initiation in research in plant physiology, anatomy, and histochemistry (PIVIC); and production of biofuels through filamentous fungi, production of substrate and enzymes from agro-industrial residues (FAPEMIG).
