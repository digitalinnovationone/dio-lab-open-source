## About me

My name is Elisângelo and I'm married to a pretty woman which attend by Laudicéia and she always have my back. We have two little children which make our home alive. We also are a happy Christian family.

I've been an enthusiast and lover of Technology since childhood due to the influence of video games. And because I believe that Technology has the power to transform people's lives, contributing to educational development and increasing the quality of life, I broke away from my already consolidated professional life (Industrial cook for 15 years) challenging myself starting in technology, where I had passed briefly through Anhanguera Educacional College(Information Systems) and afterwards, I graduated by #Trybe as Full-Stack Web Development.

I'm so thankfull God has taken my life's control carrying out everything on its time and making my childhood dream come through. I even had the chance to work and contribute to #Itaú-Unibanco's modernization by been a part of its technology's team for the last 3 years. 

At the moment I'm dedicating time to my family as I recently became father again. So I have been trying to carry out some projects to take advantage of new opportunities.
