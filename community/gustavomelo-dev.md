<h2>About Me</h2>

I'm a Full-Stack Web Developer focused on building robust and scalable technology solutions for both the Front-End and Back-End. My expertise includes modern tools and complementary No-Code development skills. I continuously strive to enhance my abilities and add value to the projects I contribute to.

With a solid foundation in **React.js**, **Next.js**, **TypeScript**, and **TailwindCSS**, alongside **.NET**, **SQL Server**, and **MongoDB**, I aim to deliver efficient applications that meet the needs of users and businesses. My experience spans not only complex systems but also the use of No-Code platforms to quickly develop automated solutions.

I'm self-taught and driven by curiosity, allowing me to tackle technical challenges with creativity and efficiency. I'm seeking opportunities that value innovation and code quality, enabling me to collaborate remotely with international teams, and I aspire to lead projects in the future.
