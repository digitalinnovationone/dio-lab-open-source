## 🔧 What I'm working on

**Mapa Gastronômico** is a mobile app developed with **Ionic + Angular** as an academic project, focused on practicing concepts of **mobile development, geolocation, interactive maps, and local storage**. <br>
It allows users to register gastronomic locations on a map created with Leaflet.js, saving the name, category, rating, comments, and date. The list has **reactive filters**, and the data is stored offline via **Ionic Storage**, without the use of external APIs.
